ABOUT US




What We Offer

As a Small School, We Have Many Things to Offer…We offer your child:

  • A safe, supportive, traditional environment. Each day begins with a flag salute and a patriotic song.
  • One on one focused attention and rigorous academic instruction.
  • Access to the latest technology – “rural” no longer means “isolated”.
  • The opportunity to be a valued member of a community where respect for others is the norm.


Safety First

The first and most fundamental place for principals to start is with physical safety.Our School is ensure the students in the door safely.

Regular Classes

Regular class attendance requires discipline and time management skills. These skills are beneficial no matter what career path you choose.

Certified Teachers

We think of teacher-heroes that taught us the academics but we don’t often think of those teachers that taught us life’s lessons.

Sufficient Classrooms

Students need to be in classrooms that inspire them and spaces that are light, airy, and filled with examples of work that they aspire to do.

Creative Lessons

Creative lesson plan should focus on the idea that with hard work, strategies, learning from mistakes, and just tinkering around, we can become more creative.

Sports Facilities

Our school sports facilities are used to deliver the formal curriculum, increase participation levels and provide facilities for the wider community.
