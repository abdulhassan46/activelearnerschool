What We Offer

As a Small School, We Have Many Things to Offer…We offer your child:

  • A safe, supportive, traditional environment. Each day begins with a flag salute and a patriotic song.
  • One on one focused attention and rigorous academic instruction.
  • Access to the latest technology – “rural” no longer means “isolated”.
  • The opportunity to be a valued member of a community where respect for others is the norm.
